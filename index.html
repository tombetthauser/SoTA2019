SotA Survey 2019

State of the Arts Survey:

  • An anonymous survey for visual artists
  • 100% open / free-to-use data after completion
  • Designed to help artists in practical decision making

State of the Arts is an anonymous census-style survey being built specifically to help artists gather and have access to critical information regarding the larger art communtity and the trends changing it. The intention of the project is to help individual artists and artist-run institutions in practical decision making.

The data gathered from the survey will be made 100% public and freely accessible for artists to draw their own conclusions and insights from. Accessibility will take the form of raw cross-referenceable data as well as targeted visualizations (graphs / charts etc).

The Beta Survey:

Before launching the official survey a shorter beta version is available for to assist in gathering general feedback and testing survey platforms. This beta survey provides an oppourtunity for interested artists to provide input for the final survey including ideas for new questions and questions that might be cut or reworded.

  • Beta Survey Closes: Fri 8/9/19

The Full Survey:

The full survey will be adjusted and added to based on the responses collected in the beta survey. Once launched it will be open for any artist to take anonymously until the end of the year with the data being made open / accessible to artists in early 2020.

  • Full Survey Opens: Mon 7/12/19
